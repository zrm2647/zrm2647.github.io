How Could I Improve

While I'm overall pleased with how my portfolio website looks right now, I do wish that I could further the design to be look even better. Being pressed for time with the project's deadline, as well as other projects I need to work on, I kept most of the design from last project and just worked to improve what was wrong last time and add what I learned since then. JavaScript has a lot of potentially interesting applications that I would've liked to add as well as learn what it can truly do. Image galleries were a bit of a pain, and didn't work with SPA (the error I noticed but was in the dark with trying to solve it). I ended up only having the default page on my projects page use an image gallery. I also wish I had more projects and other stuff that I could've added to website to flesh it out and make it look more professional.
